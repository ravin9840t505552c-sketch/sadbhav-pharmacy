Our Healthcare Services

Comprehensive pharmacy and health services delivered with warmth, professionalism, and genuine care for every patient.

OTC Medicines

A wide range of over-the-counter medicines available for common health needs — no prescription required.

Prescription Drugs

Dispensing prescription medications accurately with proper guidance. All drugs stored and handled as per DDA standards.

Minor Wound Dressing

Professional cleaning and dressing of minor cuts, wounds, and abrasions with sterile technique and quality materials.

Medical Checkups

Basic health assessments to monitor your condition and help you stay informed about your well-being.

Nebulizer Service

Blood Pressure Monitoring

Quick and accurate blood pressure checks to help you keep track of your cardiovascular health regularly.

Home Visit Services

Medication Guidance

Friendly, clear explanations on how to take medicines correctly — dosage, timing, and precautions explained with care.

Lab Specimen Collection

Collection of blood, urine, and stool samples — handled safely and forwarded to certified laboratories for accurate results.

About Us

Caring for Chitwan with Compassion & Trust

Sadbhav Pharmacy was founded with one simple belief — every person deserves accessible, honest, and compassionate healthcare. Led by Sabina Poudel, our pharmacy is a safe space where patients feel heard, respected, and well cared for.

Legally registered with the Department of Drug Administration (DDA), ensuring every medicine we dispense meets national quality standards.

Staffed by trained health professionals with practical experience in community and clinical healthcare.

Serving the local community of Pipalchowk, Bharatpur-4, Chitwan with reliable, affordable, and patient-centered healthcare services.

Special focus on home visits for geriatric and disabled patients who cannot easily travel to healthcare facilities.
